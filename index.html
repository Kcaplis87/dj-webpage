Steven Vazquez aka DJ Paisita, was born and raised in Queens,New Yorl. From a Colombian Family. He started spinning latin music in 2004 and 2 years later opened his own nightclub in Orlando, FL! Since then has been in the industry as a DJ and promoter at different nightclubs and bars in between New York and Orlando. For two years hosten his own spanish radio station and for the last 5 years has residency at Las Palmas restaurant. You may also see him doing his own shows with different latin singers in Mangos Tropical Cafe, Senor Frogs, and Colombian festivals among others!
